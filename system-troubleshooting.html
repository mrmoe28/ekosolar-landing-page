Our Comprehensive Diagnostic Process

Every EkoSolar diagnostic follows our proven 6-step methodology to ensure your solar system operates at peak efficiency and safety standards.

1

Power Source & Wiring Inspection

Comprehensive evaluation of your system's electrical foundation, ensuring safe and efficient power generation from your solar panels.

  • DC disconnect switches and safety shutoffs verification
  • Grounding system integrity and code compliance check
  • Cable condition assessment and insulation testing
  • Voltage and current measurements at source points
  • Weather protection and conduit inspection
2

Attic Run & Junction Box Analysis

Detailed inspection of internal wiring routes and connection points to identify potential issues before they cause system failures.

  • Wiring route inspection and accessibility assessment
  • Junction box connections and terminal tightness
  • Weatherproofing and environmental protection review
  • Code compliance verification and documentation
  • Heat damage and wear pattern identification
3

Roof Junction Box Assessment

Critical evaluation of rooftop electrical connections where weather exposure and thermal cycling can impact system reliability.

  • Weather sealing and gasket integrity inspection
  • Connection quality and corrosion assessment
  • Mounting security and structural evaluation
  • Performance monitoring equipment testing
  • Temperature cycling impact analysis
4

Panel Installation & Trunk Wiring

Thorough examination of solar panel mounting systems and interconnect wiring to ensure optimal energy production and structural integrity.

  • Mounting system structural integrity verification
  • Panel-to-panel connection and bypass diode testing
  • DC output verification and power curve analysis
  • Shading impact assessment and mitigation options
  • Panel alignment and tilt optimization review
5

Inverter Wiring & Commissioning Check

Advanced diagnostics of your system's power conversion equipment, ensuring efficient DC to AC conversion and grid integration.

  • AC/DC connection verification and load testing
  • Grid-tie functionality and safety testing
  • Performance optimization and efficiency analysis
  • Monitoring system calibration and data validation
  • Firmware updates and configuration optimization
6

Main Distribution Panel & Circuit Testing

Complete electrical panel assessment ensuring safe integration with your home's electrical system and compliance with all safety codes.

  • Electrical panel breaker compatibility verification
  • Load analysis and circuit capacity assessment
  • Safety disconnect and emergency shutdown testing
  • Net metering integration and bi-directional flow test
  • Electrical code compliance and permit verification
