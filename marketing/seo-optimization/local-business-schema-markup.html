EkoSolarPros Schema Markup Implementation Guide

Implementation Instructions

Add the above schema markup scripts to the <head> section of your website pages for enhanced local SEO visibility.

Key Benefits:

  • Enhanced local search visibility in Stone Mountain area searches
  • Rich snippets displaying business information, ratings, and services
  • Improved click-through rates from search results
  • Better Google My Business integration
  • FAQ rich results for common solar questions
  • Service-specific search visibility

Pages to Implement On:

  • Homepage: Full LocalBusiness schema
  • Services Pages: Service-specific schema
  • Contact Page: Organization and ContactPoint schema
  • About Page: Organization schema with team information
  • FAQ Page: FAQPage schema

Testing and Validation:

  • Use Google's Rich Results Test: https://search.google.com/test/rich-results
  • Schema.org Validator: https://validator.schema.org/
  • Monitor Google Search Console for rich result performance

Customization Notes:

  • Update URLs to match your actual website structure
  • Replace placeholder images with actual company photos
  • Customize review data with real customer reviews
  • Update geographic coordinates if business location changes
  • Add additional service areas as needed
